Job Details

Technical Sales Representative

Status: Permanent position Location: GTA North, ON

Working at CB means you are part of something big, something special!! You are part of a team of engaging, enthusiastic and dedicated people that are committed to collaborating with one another to deliver solutions to our customers.

For over 60 years, Canadian Bearings has focused on delivering products and solutions efficiently to our customers. We have become the industrial supplier of choice for many of Canada’s manufacturers because of our proven performance, continuous improvement, and ability to deliver consistent cost savings to our customers.

We are committed to seeking out skilled and energetic individuals to be our service and solutions leaders of tomorrow.

We are seeking a Technical Sales Representative

Responsibilities:

  • Meet with customers and proactively uncover their needs to support them with CB products, services and solutions to grow sales profitably
  • Promote & participate in the implementation of CB’s enterprise solutions at assigned customer sites.
  • Respond to customer's requests for technical support
  • Document all activities in Sales Force
  • Negotiate with suppliers
  • Manage existing accounts for revenue retention & increased share of wallet
  • Proactively set a strategy to develop a profitability plan and increase sales
  • Handle and resolve customer objections in a constructive, positive and timely manner
  • Negotiate and communicate price increase to customers
  • Initiate spicing according to market price in collaboration with Customer Service teams
  • Collaborate and communicate with Operations teams on business opportunities

Required Technical Skills, Knowledge & Attributes in Action:

  • Knowledge of the MRO industry, products & services is mandatory
  • Strong knowledge and application of sales process and skills - e.g. selling total cost, negotiation, closing, presenting, handling objections
  • Strong knowledge of sales processes & tools - CRM applications, Microsoft Office
  • Knowledge of sales management processes, reports
  • Good general business knowledge, business concepts and basic business finance
  • Excellent verbal, written and presentation communication skills in English and French
  • Ability to easily build and advance productive relationships – excellent listening skills, consults with others
  • Strong goal setting, planning and prioritizing ability
  • Reasons with data, assesses results against objectives, and applies creative thinking
  • Initiate spicing according to market price in collaboration with Customer Service teams
  • Driven to achieve

Required Experience and Education:

  • College or University Degree in a business or technical discipline
  • At least 5 years working experience in sales and or sales management

A few reasons why it's great to work for us!

  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Comprehensive medical and dental benefits
  • RRSP matching plan
  • Personal Development Subsidy
  • Opportunities for growth and development

Canadian Bearings is an equal opportunity employer.
